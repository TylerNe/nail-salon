About Us

Where luxury meets exceptional service

Welcome to Precision Nails & Beauty

Located in a beautiful corner in Noarlunga, SA 5168, Precision Nails & Beauty is the ideal destination for anyone who wants to get pampered. Our nail salon takes pride in providing our valued customers with all good services and customer service satisfaction.

We have a wide range of services such as Manicures, Pedicures, Facials, Tinting, Lashes, Waxing, Massage, and so much more. Our highly skilled nail artists are trained to provide what you deserve.

Every visit to our salon is designed to be a moment of relaxation and self-care. From the moment you walk through our doors, you'll be treated to personalized attention and professional service that exceeds your expectations.
